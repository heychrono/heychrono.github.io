My methods for creating functional digital products

This design process has provided me reliable & effective ways to improve my work efficiency. Occasionally, it’s going smoothly, but at other time, it’s deviating. Still, I manage to create a new route to get back on the right track.

1

Empathize

Understand the problem by setting your own beliefs aside to gain a real insight into your users and their needs.

2

Define

Synthesize and analyze the data you have gathered to define your problem statement

3

Ideate

Brainstorm ways to formulate innovative solutions to address the problem statement.

4

Prototype

Get your ideas into a low-fidelity version of the intended product to solve each problem found and improve it over time.

5

Test

Seek to get feedback on whether or not your prototype solves the problem of the people who might use the actual product one day.
